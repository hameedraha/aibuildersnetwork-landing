a network of builders

ai builders network is a gathering of people who ship AI-powered products, tools, and experiments. if you're wiring models to APIs at midnight or testing prompts on a side project — you belong here.

who it's for

software engineers, indie founders, and curious makers who want peers, not an audience. people who care more about what ships than what sounds impressive.

how it works

weekly build demos. async knowledge sharing. early access to each other's projects. accountability through showing up and sharing your work, rough edges included.

why now

the tools changed in 2024. what one person can ship alone changed too. a small, focused group of serious builders compounds faster than any large, passive audience.

why join

the reasons are concrete.

peers who build

skip the theory. talk with people who shipped something this week and have the commits to prove it.

shared knowledge

real findings from real experiments — what model handles what task, what breaks under load, what actually makes it to users.

early idea access

see projects before they're polished. give feedback that shapes what gets built, not what gets announced.

built-in accountability

weekly demos keep you honest. nothing ships faster than knowing your peers will ask "so, what did you build this week?"

who belongs here

filter yourself in.
or out.

you'll thrive here if —
  • you're building something with AI, however early or rough
  • you're an engineer, founder, or maker who experiments hands-on
  • you want peers, not a platform to broadcast at
  • you care more about shipping than being right in a thread
  • you're comfortable sharing half-finished ideas in public
this isn't for you if —
  • you want to spectate without contributing
  • you need polished content and finished answers only
  • you're not ready to show your work publicly
