Everyone is building apps. very few are building products people actually want. this workshop teaches you how to go from idea to product to customer using modern ai tools and proven startup thinking.

You won’t watch someone build a to-do app in 15 minutes. you’ll learn how to find real problems, validate them, ship with ai, and take your product to market.

the four phases

  1. identify — find real problems worth solving, not problems you invent to use a cool tool.
  2. validate — pressure-test customer pain points before you write a single prompt.
  3. build — use ai tools to ship a minimum lovable product, not just an mvp.
  4. launch — position, price, and get your first real customers.

what you walk away with

  • lovable credits (300) — build and iterate without hitting free-plan limits
  • prompt library + build stack — proven prompts, design refs, n8n templates
  • sayaboutus credits — collect fast product feedback
  • ai builders network access — private builder community
  • physical launch certificate + vibe coder swag

who should attend

founders, pms, developers, students, freelancers, and operators building with ai. you have an idea and want to turn it into a real product. you’re ready for hands-on work, not passive theory.

what to bring

just your laptop, an idea you’re excited to build, and 4 focused hours. snacks and refreshments included.
