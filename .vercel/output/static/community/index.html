what is this community.

a small, close-knit group of engineers, founders, and makers who ship AI-powered products, tools, and experiments. weekly build demos. async knowledge sharing. peers, not an audience.

what you get

out of joining.

peers who build

talk with people who shipped something this week and have the commits to prove it.

shared knowledge

real findings from real experiments — what works, what breaks, what actually ships.

early idea access

see projects before they're polished. give feedback that shapes what gets built.

built-in accountability

weekly demos keep you honest. your peers will ask "so, what did you build this week?"

who it's for

and who it's not.

for you if —
  • you're building something with AI, however early or rough
  • you're an engineer, founder, or maker who experiments hands-on
  • you want peers, not a platform to broadcast at
  • you care more about shipping than being right in a thread
not for you if —
  • you want to spectate without contributing
  • you need polished content and finished answers only
  • you're not ready to show your work publicly
